About Me

Hi, I'm Jennifer Morrison, a passionate web designer and developer based in Raleigh, NC. With a strong background in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I specialize in creating functional and accessible web pages that provide an exceptional user experience.

I am dedicated to continuous learning and staying up-to-date with the latest industry trends to ensure my work is innovative and effective.

My Path in Web Development

My interest in web development grew out of a lifelong passion for design and storytelling. As a high school yearbook editor and an avid scrapbooker, I have always enjoyed organizing information visually and creating meaningful layouts that communicate clearly. During our homeschool journey, when my son enrolled in a web development course, it sparked my curiosity about how design and technology come together to build interactive experiences. What began as exploration quickly developed into a commitment to learning front-end development, where I discovered a passion for creating accessible, responsive websites that balance creativity with functionality.

Through a variety of projects and collaborative work, I’ve refined my ability to balance design and development while solving complex challenges. I enjoy connecting ideas, code, and creativity to create websites that are both functional and meaningful.

I’m excited for opportunities to collaborate and build solutions where each piece contributes to a cohesive final result.
